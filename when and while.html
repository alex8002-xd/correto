    • When and While


    When and while se usa para expresar relaciones de tiempo, causa y efecto, contraste y condición.


    Ejemplos:


    To refer to a specific moment in time:
    I was eating when the phone rang.
    (Estaba comiendo cuando sonó el teléfono.)



    To talk about situations that happen simultaneously or at the same moment:
    She was happy when she heard the news.
    (Ella estaba feliz cuando escuchó la noticia.)


    To refer to situations in which one action happens after another:
    I´ll call you when i arrive.
    (Te llamaré cuando llegue.)

    While
